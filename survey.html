🔧 Hardware / Sound Settings Checklist
  1. Check physical mic mute button — headsets and webcams often have a hardware mute button. Make sure it's not pressed.
  2. Right-click the speaker icon → Open Sound Settings → under Input, confirm the correct microphone is selected.
  3. In Sound Settings click "Device properties" under Input — make sure Disable is NOT ticked.
  4. Click "Additional device properties" → Levels tab → drag microphone volume to 80–100.
  5. Open mmsys.cpl (Run → Win+R) → Recording tab → right-click your mic → Set as Default Device.
  6. Check Device Manager for yellow warning icons on audio devices — reinstall driver if present.
  7. Try a different browser (Chrome or Edge). Firefox sometimes needs extra codec support.
  8. Try a different microphone / headset to rule out hardware failure.
